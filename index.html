About the Coalition

Our mission is to build an international, multi‐stakeholder coalition dedicated to ensuring that AI technologies not only innovate but also support the cognitive, emotional, and social development of children. Bringing together governments, tech companies, educators, researchers, and families, we are working to shape a future where every child benefits from ethical and responsible AI.
