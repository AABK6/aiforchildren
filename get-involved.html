Shape the Future of AI for Children: Join Our Coalition

Become a part of a global movement dedicated to ensuring AI benefits every child. Learn how you can contribute to the Beneficial AI for Children Coalition.

The Beneficial AI for Children Coalition is a multi-stakeholder initiative committed to fostering a future where Artificial Intelligence supports children's cognitive, emotional, and social development. We believe that by working together, we can guide the evolution of AI to ensure it empowers and protects the youngest members of our global community.

We invite governments, tech companies, educational institutions, research bodies, civil society organizations, and individuals passionate about child welfare and technology to join us. Your involvement is crucial in shaping the ethical and responsible development of AI for the benefit of children worldwide.

Why Join the Beneficial AI for Children Coalition?

Joining the Coalition means becoming part of a powerful international network dedicated to making a tangible difference in children's lives. As a member, you will:

  • Influence Global Guidelines: Directly contribute to the development of ethical guidelines and best practices for AI in children's contexts.
  • Collaborate with Leaders: Engage with governments, tech industry leaders, and academic experts to drive impactful change.
  • Access Exclusive Insights: Gain early access to research findings, policy discussions, and innovative projects shaping the field.
  • Amplify Your Impact: Leverage the collective voice of the Coalition to advocate for policies and initiatives that prioritize children in the age of AI.
  • Shape a Positive Future: Play a pivotal role in ensuring AI technologies are a force for good in the development and well-being of future generations.

Explore Ways to Get Involved

There are multiple avenues to contribute to the Beneficial AI for Children Coalition, depending on your organization type or individual capacity:

For Organizations:

  • Partnership: Formalize your commitment by becoming an official partner. Engage in collaborative projects, contribute to policy development, and help set the strategic direction of the Coalition.
  • Sponsorship: Support our operational activities, research initiatives, and global events through financial or in-kind contributions.
  • Expertise Sharing: Offer your organization's unique expertise in AI development, child psychology, education, or policy to enrich our collective knowledge.

For Individuals:

  • Become a Community Member: Subscribe to our newsletter for regular updates, participate in public forums and webinars, and help disseminate our message through your networks.
  • Volunteer: Contribute your time and skills to specific projects, working groups, or outreach activities. Contact us to learn about current volunteer opportunities.
  • Researchers and Experts: Share your research, data, and insights to inform our evidence-based approach. Participate in research collaborations and expert panels.
