Why Join the Coalition?

By joining the Beneficial AI for Children Coalition, you become part of an international network of governments, institutions, tech companies, researchers, educators, and families. Together, we are dedicated to ensuring that AI technologies support the cognitive, emotional, and social development of every child.

Working as one, we can influence the development of ethical, inclusive, and age-appropriate AI systems—shaping a future where technology empowers and protects our children.

How Can You Get Involved?

  • Organizations: Partner with us to help shape guidelines, share expertise, and implement best practices for child‐focused AI.
  • Individuals: Subscribe for updates, join our discussions, and help spread the word about our mission.
  • Experts & Researchers: Contribute your insights and evidence‐based research to guide our ongoing work and policy recommendations.
